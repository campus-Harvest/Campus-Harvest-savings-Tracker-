Program Rules & Transparency

90-Day Duration

The savings program runs for exactly 90 days from your registration date.

Daily Contribution

Each member contributes a fixed amount daily. The exact amount is communicated upon registration.

Missed Payments

Missed payments can be made up within 7 days without penalty. After that, a small fee applies.

Withdrawal Rules

Full withdrawal is available after completing 90 days. Partial withdrawals have a 5% processing fee.

Admin Contact

For issues: Email savings@campus.edu or WhatsApp +234 902 864 0574(Mon-Fri, 9am-5pm).

Data Security

Your data is securely stored and only accessible to you and authorized program administrators.
